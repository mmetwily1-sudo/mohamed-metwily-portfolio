The Challenge

Alwfi.com operates in the highly competitive Saudi automotive spare parts market, dominated by established players with strong brand recognition. The goal was to build organic visibility from scratch and drive measurable revenue growth.

The Strategy

Implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy focusing on high-intent commercial keywords, technical optimization for e-commerce product pages, and AI Overviews visibility to capture search demand at the moment of decision.

Key Results

  • Organic search became the #1 revenue channel with 71.9% of total store revenue (633,727 SAR out of 881,076 SAR)
  • Generated 95.7K organic clicks and 2.54M impressions via Google Search Console
  • Achieved top 1-5 search rankings for high-intent commercial keywords in the Saudi market
  • Secured visibility in Google AI Overviews for key product-related search queries
  • Achieved average CTR of 3.8% and average search position of 5.5
  • Delivered 1,156 conversions from organic search (72.6% of all conversions)
  • Grew organic sessions to 1.06M (74.7% of total traffic)

The Challenge

Hishma-sa.com is a modest fashion and jalabiyat e-commerce store targeting the Saudi market. The goal was to increase organic visibility and drive high-intent traffic that converts, in a market with strong competition from both local and international fashion brands.

The Strategy

Focused on category-level SEO optimization, content strategy for product descriptions, and building topical authority in the modest fashion niche. Leveraged AI Overviews to capture both commercial and informational search intent.

Key Results

  • Organic search generated 618,817 SAR in revenue (21% of total 2.95M SAR)
  • Achieved the highest conversion rate among all channels at 13.67%
  • Drove 1.74M organic sessions with average engagement time of 3+ minutes per session
  • Achieved average CTR of 6.5% and average search position of 4.9
  • Organic search engagement rate reached 89.5%, significantly outperforming paid channels
  • Secured visibility in Google AI Overviews for commercial and informational queries

The Challenge

MHS Jewellery is a heritage jewelry brand established in 1997, specializing in prayer beads (سبح), precious stones, and silver items. The challenge was to build organic visibility for a traditional brand entering the e-commerce space in the Saudi market.

The Strategy

Created a comprehensive SEO strategy combining traditional product optimization with modern e-commerce best practices. Focused on building topical authority in the prayer beads and precious stones niche.

Key Results

  • Organic search became the #1 revenue channel with 43.8% of total revenue (21,340 SAR out of 48,768 SAR)
  • Outperformed direct traffic (18.8%) by more than 2x
  • Generated 4.32K organic clicks and 242K impressions via Google Search Console
  • Achieved average CTR of 1.8% and average search position of 10.7
  • Organic search delivered 40 conversions with 0.72% conversion rate
  • Successfully established online presence for a heritage brand in the digital space
